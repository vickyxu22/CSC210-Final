EU email communication network

The network was generated using email data from a large European research institution. 
Anonymized information about all incoming and outgoing email of the EU research institution from October 2003 to May 2005 (18 months).


* source url: https://snap.stanford.edu/data/email-EuAll.html
* authors/owners: J. Leskovec, J. Kleinberg and C. Faloutsos. 

* number of nodes: 265214
* number of edges: 420045

* Qualities of the graph:  directed; skip self-edges; no duplicate edges.
